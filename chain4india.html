CHAIN 4 INDIA

Supplychain Model
for PPE kits.

Manufacturers

Manufactures Will Add Record of Equipments like PPE Kits when it is Manufactured.

Certifying Authorities

Certifying Authorities will be able to Change Certification Status of PPE Kits.

Supplychain Transporters

Supply Chain Transporters are All Kinds of middle men like Distributers, Wholesellers, Retailers who will be participating in transportation of that equipments.

Hospitals

Hospitals should join this supply chain and get benifits of Trust , Tracebility and Transperncy of Medical equipments like PPE kits.
